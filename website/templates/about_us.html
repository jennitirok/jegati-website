About Us


PT Jegati Gempita Trijaya was established on August, 2019, specialized in supplying Pharmaceutical and Cosmetic raw materials certified by BPOM (National Agency of Drug and Food Control) and Ministry of Health Indonesia

Approved by PBF (Pedagang Besar Farmasi) and CDOB (Cara Distribusi Obat yang Baik), we have ensured that our facilities, infrastructure and quality of products have qualified the highest standard.

We are committed to maintain our best standard as a supplier through our strong cooperation with manufacturers from all around the world.
